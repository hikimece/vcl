ECE9048 Linux Systems and Lab


Course Description
This course is for second-year students in the School of Electronics and Computer Engineering, and there are no official prerequisites. However, experience with basic programming (such as C/C++ or Python) can help understand the lectures and for programming in the lab. Please find the syllabus here in detail.
- Instructor: Prof. Hyung-Il Kim, School of Electronics and Computer Engineering
- Lecture Room: Engineering Bldg. 6, #720 (공6-720호)
