Welcome to our lab!

The Visual Computing & Learning (VCL) Laboratory at the Chonnam National University conducts cutting-edge research in visual intelligence technologies. Our work primarily focuses on image recognition and understanding, supported by advancements in deep learning and machine learning. Recently, we have been particularly interested in improving the generalization of visual intelligence models and exploring the utilization and training of large-scale multimodal and vision-language models. Our ultimate goal is to apply these foundational technologies to various vision applications, contributing to the development of machine intelligence for human convenience and, ultimately, laying the groundwork for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
